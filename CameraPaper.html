CameraPaper
the `Live' wallpaper experience.
Project page: http://github.com/itszero/CameraPaper

Features

  • Tranparentize your Android phone
  • Double-Tap to turn camera off
  • Triple-Tap to snap a photo
  • (Pro) Record video right from the Launcher
